Press START to place bulbs in a circle. Bulbs that are lit are yellow,

the randomized winning bulb is red and the rest are green.

Try to stop the cycle of flashing bulbs on the winning bulb with the STOP button!

You can use the RESET button to reload the page, e.g. to try again after pressing STOP.

Before pressing START you can adjust the CYCLE SPEED and the

AMOUNT OF BULBS with the sliders provided.
